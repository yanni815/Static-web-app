    Brook

    Anime: One piece

    Role: Straw hat pirate and musician

    Species:Skeleton

    Ablities: Yomi-Yomi no Mi(Revive-Revive fruit), Swordsman

    First Apperance: Episode 337

    Backstory

    When Brook was alive he was a member of the Rumbar Pirates. But when his crew was attacked by rival pirates, he succumbed to posion while playing the captains favorite song. His soul consummed the Yomi-Yomi no Mi which brought him back to life, but his soul took so long to find his body. So he came back as a skeleton with only his afro in tact.

    Apperance

    Brook stands at 8'8 1/2 ft tall. Despite being a skeleton he has an afro because he has strong roots according to him. He dresses like a dapper individual wearing a full suit and top hat. He also carries a purple cane which doubles as his sword. After the timeskip he grows to 9'1 and adorns heart sunglasses, adds a crown to his hat. He also wears a feather boa and orangy-red pants.

    Personality

    Ecentric, lewd, soft-spoken, polite. Brook's title of the Soul King shows how much compassion he has for his crew. He has emotional depth and a deep sense of compassion.
